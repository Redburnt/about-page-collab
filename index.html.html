About us

Welcome to the Aramide Ojo Foundation (A.O.F.), a place where education meets compassion, and every child's potential is nurtured to flourish. Founded by Chief Mrs. Otunba Aramide Simisola Ojo, A.O.F. is a non-profit organization in Nigeria dedicated to transforming lives through education, digital literacy, and mentorship.

At A.O.F., we believe that every child deserves to be seen, loved, and empowered—regardless of their background or circumstances. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap by providing accessible, high-quality learning opportunities that go beyond traditional classrooms, preparing young minds for a future filled with endless possibilities.

"...because every child deserves to know and believe that they are SEEN & LOVED."

Who we are

A.O.F is a non-profit organization committed to transforming the lives of children and young adults through education, technology, and mentorship. Our foundation stands as a pillar of hope, ensuring that every child, regardless of background or circumstance, receives the support they need to excel.

Built on the belief that every child deserves to be seen, valued, and loved, our mission is to bridge the gap between quality education and underprivileged communities, empowering young minds to dream big and achieve their full potential.

At A.O.F, we are not just shaping students—we are nurturing future leaders, innovators, and changemakers.

What we do

We provide a wide range of educational and empowerment programs aimed at equipping children and young adults with the skills they need to succeed.

At the Aramide Ojo Foundation (A.O.F), we are committed to transforming lives through education, empowerment, and community development. Our initiatives go beyond traditional learning, fostering an environment where children and young minds can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Through a holistic approach, we equip them with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to build a brighter future.
